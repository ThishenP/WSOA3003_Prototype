DOT FIGHTER

How To Play

Primary Fire - Left Mouse Button (LMB)

Secondary Fire - Right Mouse Button (RMB)

Bomb - Middle Mouse Button (MMB)

Pause - Space (sound settings in pause menu)

Avoid or kill incoming red dots

Avoid bright red walls
